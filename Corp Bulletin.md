# Welcome, new citizen, to the WOMPire!
In this document, we will present all the information you need to find your way around your new home. Should you have questions about any of the points presented here, feel free to ask them in the #newbros channel on Discord.

## Mumble
To communicate during fleets or have a chat while hanging out in space, we use Mumble. You can download the software here: https://www.mumble.info/downloads/
To set up Mumble for your account, go to  https://auth.wompa.space/services/ then follow these steps:

1. Under "Mumble" click the yellow box with the white checkmark:
   <img width="353" height="354" alt="Mumble01" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7cfa3fe4-42ed-4c92-a14a-10a6b0e5efb2" />
2. Make a note of your Username and Password, then click "Continue":
   <img width="757" height="380" alt="Mumble02" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1769b395-b41e-4081-b181-a9e5aa33f02e" />
3. Under "Mumble" click the green box with the arrow:
   <img width="356" height="358" alt="Mumble03" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3b102486-9e97-48ad-b0f6-8f6afcabbc3a" />
   a. There might be a browser pop up asking if mumble links should be opened. If so, click "Open Link":
      <img width="421" height="197" alt="Mumble03a" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2458440-623e-4ded-bd80-f21a5afa399c" />
4. After connecting to the WOMP Mumble server, Mumble will ask for the password that was generated in step 2. Enter the password and click "OK":
   <img width="389" height="184" alt="Mumble04" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10b72d9c-fd4e-40db-949a-ab75e1de6917" />
5. Once the connection is successful, in the top left, click on "Server", then on "Connect":
   <img width="195" height="290" alt="Mumble05" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eedadf4c-93ed-4374-af0a-9296cd980272" />
6. In the pop up window, at the bottom, click "Add New...":
   <img width="576" height="400" alt="Mumble06" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/187e1031-384e-404c-ac6b-7ce3c199bb6d" />
7. In the new pop up window, in the top right, click "Fill":
   <img width="434" height="316" alt="Mumble07" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b08825b4-ed5e-44a6-a002-d7c12b10a611" />
8. All the information should be filled out now, click "OK":
   <img width="431" height="226" alt="Mumble08" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f9e5c0d4-583e-4333-b16b-6f1b51b6ab85" />
9. Close the pop up window from point 6. From now on, whenever Mumble starts, it should automatically connect to the WOMP Mumble Server. 

Now that the server connection is done, you can move to setting up the two keys you need to talk on Mumble. The **Push-To-Talk** key is used to communicate to all channels in your current channel group, useful when you're in a fleet and FC is in the Command channel and you're in the DPS channel. The **Whisper/Shout** key is used to talk to other members who are in the same channel as you. Unless you're an FC, you will mostly use the **Whisper/Shout** key. To set both of these keys up, follow this short video:
https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52bdb530-c722-45d9-a6d3-38fb322c0d71

## Home System
Our current staging system is **3-FKCZ** with our home station, the **3-FKCZ - W O M P S T A R**. This is the starting point for the majority of our PVP fleets and is also our local market hub. Once you're docked in the WOMPSTAR, make sure to right click its name in the top left and set it as your Home Station!
