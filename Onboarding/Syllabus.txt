# Page 5 - Prop Mod
<u>Prop Mod on/off</u>
- Propulsion Module: MWD or AB
- Keep yourself in position with fleet by matching anchor's call outs

<b>~~» Prop mod on! «~~</b>
--- Anyone falling behind? ---
<b>~~» Prop mod off! «~~</b>

<u>Pulse Prop Mod</u>
- Activate your prop mod for a single cycle
- Active modules (like props) cycle, giving benefits while active
- Green outline on module's icon indicates cycle is active normally, red outline indicates module is de-cycling (queued to stop)
- Pulsing for multiple cycles means you must count off the cycles yourself before de-activating
- Pulse as needed if falling behind fleet!

<u>When to Broadcast for Reps</u>
- Either early or late
___ <font size="12" color="#ffffe400"><a href="http://i.imgur.com/iod3V47.png">When to Broadcast for Reps</a></font> ___
- In-game yellow box indicates you are locked, red-box indicates you are aggressed
- Ideally, broadcast when mass yellow-boxed (early)

<u>When not to Broadcast for Reps</u>
- When flying frigates in larger fleets, or when taking bomb damage
- Frigates take a long time for Logi to lock and have low HP
  - Usually not worth broadcasting
- Better to warp off and warp immediately back to anchor
  - Choose a nearby celestial, use Wachtlist to Warp to Member back on anchor
- Only Broadcast for bomb damage if FC calls for it

<u>Jump Cloak Timer</u>
- Jumping gates gives a 60 second invul/invis timer

<b>==» Hold Cloak, then break manually «==</b>

- Listen to FC for whether to break or hold jump cloak
- While jump cloak active (icon at top-left window) you are unable to be locked, unable to take damage, and invisible on grid
- Cloak breaks whenever you move, or when timer expires

<u>Commands After Gate Jumps</u>
- If Enemies on Grid, FC may say to:
  - <i>Break Cloak (and Start Shooting)</i>: move (either double-click in space or align somewhere) and start targeting enemies
- If bubbled, FC may say to:
  - <i>Crash the Gate</i>: re-approach with prop mod on and jump
  - <i>Burn Out of Bubble</i>: manually pilot (double click) to closest edge of bubble, turn your prop mod on until you are out of the bubble
